About Me

I am Sahil Singh, a passionate social worker committed to driving meaningful change at the grassroots level. Through community organizing, educational outreach, and sustainable development projects, I strive to create inclusive spaces where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

My journey as a samajsevi (social servant) is rooted in the belief that collective action and empathy are the most powerful tools for building a just and equitable society.

My Initiatives

Key projects and campaigns I have led or contributed to in service of the community.

Education for All

Running free tuition centers and scholarship programs for underprivileged children to ensure no child is left behind.

Women Empowerment

Skill development workshops and self-help groups enabling women to achieve financial independence and social dignity.

Health & Hygiene

Organizing medical camps, blood donation drives, and awareness campaigns on sanitation and preventive healthcare.

Environmental Action

Tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and advocacy for sustainable practices in local communities.

Youth Leadership

Mentoring young leaders through workshops and forums that encourage civic engagement and community responsibility.

Digital Literacy

Bridging the digital divide by teaching basic computer skills and internet literacy to rural and underserved populations.
